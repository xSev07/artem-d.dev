Unable to complete the operation

The operation was not completed due to a technical error.

What this means

In rare cases, the local database or the device operating system may temporarily prevent data from being written.

What you can try

  • Close and reopen the app
  • Restart your device
  • Check that there is enough free storage on your device
  • Make sure the latest version of the app is installed

If the problem persists

Contact support:
support@artem-d.dev

In your email, please include:

  • device model
  • Android version
  • app version
  • what you were doing before the error occurred
